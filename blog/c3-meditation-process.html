How did Process over Product affect the way you tackled the site?

I felt this concept helped me know when to stop working on the site, for right now, even if it feels ugly or unfinished. I understood that the point was not to have an incredible portfolio site, but to learn about frameworks and grids and try them out through the medium of my portfolio site. I, personally, am unhappy with the aesthetics of my site. It looks way too amateurish and child-like to me. But through this I learned more about frameworks, and the grid style class, and flex boxes, and so on. I also learned of things I didn't have time to do (like get good at making an SVG). It was hard to not just tinker on my site forever, or to avoid feelings of disatisfaction when comparing the site to other portfolios....but I remembered i'm in week 3 of my 18 week process, and I have time to learn, refine, and make something I'm proud of. This epiphany helped me finish the week in a way I was proud of.
