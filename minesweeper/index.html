How to Play

Beneath each square in the grid above lies either a "friendly space" or a mine. Friendly spaces want to see the light of day; mines blow you up.

The Objective!

Uncover all friendly spaces, and mark all mines with an exclamation point.

Controls

Left Mouse Click Uncover a square. IF it's a mine, you blow up. If the square is friendly, it will have a number stating how many mines this square touches.

Right Mouse Click Marks a space with an exclamation point. This is good for remembering where the mines be.
